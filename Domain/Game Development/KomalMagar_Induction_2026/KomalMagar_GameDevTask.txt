# Game Development Task - Enigma Induction 2026
**Name: Komal Magar**

---

## Objective: Beginner Environment Design
**Category:** Fantasy Forest
**Theme:** "The Whispering Woods" - A mysterious, lush forest with glowing flora.

---

## Design Concepts:
The environment is designed to create an immersive and mystical atmosphere:
1. **Lush Greenery**: Used high-density trees and ferns from the Unity Asset Store to create a sense of depth and mystery.
2. **Glowing Flora**: Placed custom-lit mushrooms and glowing vines to guide the viewer's eye through the scene.
3. **Lighting**: Implemented a "Volumetric Lighting" (God Rays) effect to simulate sunlight filtering through the dense canopy.
4. **Water Elements**: Added a calm, reflecting pond in the center to provide a sense of peace.

---

## Asset Usage:
- **Environment Assets**: "Unity Terrain Tools", "Free Nature Assets", and "Fantasy Forest Asset Pack".
- **Lighting**: Custom Point Lights and Directional Light with volumetric settings.
- **VFX**: Particle systems for falling leaves and fireflies.
