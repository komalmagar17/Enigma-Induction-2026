## Design Screen Description:
The designed screen is the **"Product Detail"** page of the Caffeina mobile app:
- **Brand Identity**: Features the Caffeina logo and a minimalist color palette of deep espresso browns and cream.
- **Visual Hierarchy**: High-quality product image at the top, followed by the product name, price, and a short description.
- **Interactive Elements**: Large, rounded "Add to Cart" button with a smooth hover/press effect.
- **Icons**: Simple, line-art icons for "Favorites", "Share", and "Eco-Friendly Certification".

---

## Interaction & Motion:
I have added the following interactions using Figma Prototype:
- **Button Navigation**: Clicking on the product takes you to the cart preview.
- **Hover Effects**: The "Add to Cart" button changes color slightly on hover.
- **Screen Transitions**: Smooth slide-in transition between the home and product detail screens.
- **Simple Animations**: A gentle fade-in animation for the product image.
