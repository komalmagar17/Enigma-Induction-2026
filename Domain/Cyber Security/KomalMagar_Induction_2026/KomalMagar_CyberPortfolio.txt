## Challenges Faced:
1. **VirtualBox VT-x Error**: Encountered a "VT-x is disabled in the BIOS" error. Solved by restarting the computer, entering BIOS settings, and enabling "Intel Virtualization Technology".
2. **GoBuster Wordlist Location**: Was unsure where the default wordlists were located. Used `locate wordlists` to find the correct path in `/usr/share/wordlists/`.
3. **Network Connection**: Initially, Kali had no internet. Switched the Network adapter setting in VirtualBox from "NAT" to "Bridged Adapter" to allow direct access.
